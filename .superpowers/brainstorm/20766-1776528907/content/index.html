Products tab is one unified view — no sub-tabs.
1Filter tabs (All / Failed / Warnings / Synced) replace the old Products / Compliance / AI sub-tabs. One place to see everything.
2Each row shows GMC status + Meta status + AI toggle. Errors and warnings surface inline under the product name — no clicking through to a separate compliance screen.
3Clicking "Fix" expands the row in-place with a three-panel detail: fix inputs (with Apply to Wix / GMC / Both), current feed values, and AI enhancement toggle/button for that product.
4SyncStream AI toggle per product lives in the table — merchant can enable/disable per product without leaving the list view.
